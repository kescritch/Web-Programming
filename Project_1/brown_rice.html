Brown Rice

  • Richer in fiber
  • White rice undergoes more processing, having its husk, bran, and germ removed, while brown rice only has its outer husk removed.
  • Brown rice has a chewier texture and a nuttier flavor, while white rice is softer and more neutral in taste.
  • White rice has a longer shelf life due to the removal of the bran and germ, which contain oils that can turn rancid, whereas brown rice spoils faster.
